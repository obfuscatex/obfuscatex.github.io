Privacy Policy

Last Updated: April 8, 2026

Summary: We respect your privacy. Your reading progress is stored only on your device and never transmitted to us. We use the Meta Audience Network to display non-personalized ads, which do not track your behavior for targeted advertising.

1. Introduction

Welcome to Bible - Chapter By Chapter ("we," "our," or "us"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Information Stored Locally

The following data is stored only on your device and is never transmitted to us:

  • Reading progress (chapters completed)
  • Quiz scores and history
  • Notification preferences and reminder settings
  • Reading streaks

2.2 Information Collected by Third Parties

We use the Meta Audience Network to display advertisements. To respect your privacy, these ads are strictly non-personalized and do not track your specific behavior to serve custom ads. Meta may still collect limited technical information, such as:

  • Device advertising identifiers (for frequency capping, aggregated reporting, and fraud/invalid click prevention)
  • IP address (anonymized/truncated)
  • App usage statistics and interactions with the ad
  • Crash logs & diagnostics
  • Device information (model, OS version)

For more information on how Meta uses your data, please review:

  • Meta's Privacy Policy
  • Meta Audience Network Policy

3. How We Use Information

  • Local Data: To save your progress and provide a personalized reading experience
  • Advertising Data: To display contextual, non-personalized advertisements to support the free app

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or share your personal information with third parties, except:

  • Meta Audience Network (for non-personalized advertising purposes, as described above)
  • When required by law or legal process

5. Data Security

Your reading progress and preferences are stored securely on your device using Android's DataStore. We do not have access to this data.

6. Children's Privacy

Our app is suitable for all ages. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The app displays strictly non-personalized ads for all users, completely avoiding any behavioral tracking.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Delete your data: Clear app data from your device settings or uninstall the app
  • Disable notifications: Turn off reminders in the app's Settings screen

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.
